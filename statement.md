\# Project Statement: Monthly Expense Tracker



\## Problem Statement

Students and young professionals need a simple way to track daily expenses and manage monthly budgets effectively. Manual tracking in notebooks or spreadsheets is time-consuming and error-prone.



\## Scope of the Project



\### What's Included:

\- Add, view, and manage daily expenses

\- Categorize spending (Food, Transport, Entertainment, Bills, etc.)

\- Monthly expense summaries and reports

\- Basic budget tracking

\- Data storage in local files



\### What's Not Included:

\- User accounts or authentication

\- Mobile application

\- Bank account integration

\- Advanced investment tracking

\- Multi-currency support



\## Target Users

1\. \*\*College Students\*\* - Managing monthly allowances and expenses

2\. \*\*Young Professionals\*\* - Tracking daily spending habits

3\. \*\*Budget-Conscious Individuals\*\* - Monitoring where money goes each month



\## High-Level Features



\### Core Functionality:

\- \*\*Add New Expenses\*\* - Record amount, category, description, and date

\- \*\*View All Expenses\*\* - See complete expense history

\- \*\*Category-wise Summary\*\* - View spending by category (Food, Transport, etc.)

\- \*\*Monthly Reports\*\* - Get overview of monthly spending patterns

\- \*\*Data Persistence\*\* - Automatically save and load expense data



\### User Experience:

\- Simple menu-driven interface

\- Easy input validation

\- Clear expense displays

\- Instant total calculations
